Button colours

There are seven standard button colours. 'Default' and 'Primary' are the most used - 'Primary' for main (primary) actions, and 'Default' for secondary and less important actions in the context of any given UI. 'Success' is often used for positive actions (e.g. a 'save' action). 'Danger' is often used for a negative action (e.g. a 'delete' or 'cancel' action).
